About Auxilia

Auxilia began as a student initiative to help underfunded non-profits in Bengaluru. We offer volunteer matching, social media support, supply drives, and small micro-grants. Our projects focus on sustainability, transparency, and student leadership.

See Projects Volunteer

Our Mission

To create a replicable student-led model that supports grassroots NGOs with volunteers, resources, and capacity-building tools. We prioritise long-term partnerships and measurable outcomes.

How we work

  • Partner with small NGOs and co-design short-term projects
  • Run volunteer drives and training sessions
  • Support NGOs with digital outreach and micro-fundraising

Selected Projects

Volunteer Match

Trained student volunteers placed with partner NGOs for monthly shifts — education, feeding programs, and logistics.

Supply Drives

Coordinated donation drives for books, clothes, and food with transparent distribution reports.

Digital Helpdesk

Helping NGOs build basic social media presence and simple donor pages to increase visibility.

Youth Fellowship

A micro-fellowship program where student volunteers work with NGOs on defined 4-week projects.
